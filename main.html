Naruto Uzumaki

From: Naruto

Naruto Uzumaki is a young ninja who dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. Despite being ostracized by his peers, Naruto works hard to gain their respect and protect his friends.

Monkey D. Luffy

From: One Piece

Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. He aims to find the legendary treasure known as One Piece and become the Pirate King. His rubber body, gained from eating a Devil Fruit, gives him unique abilities.

Goku

From: Dragon Ball

Goku is a Saiyan warrior who protects Earth from various threats. He trains constantly to become stronger and has saved the planet multiple times from powerful enemies.

Light Yagami

From: Death Note

Light Yagami is a brilliant student who discovers a mysterious notebook that can kill anyone whose name is written in it. He uses it to rid the world of criminals, but his methods are morally questionable.

Eren Yeager

From: Attack on Titan

Eren Yeager vows to eradicate the Titans after they destroy his hometown and kill his mother. He possesses the ability to transform into a Titan himself, which he uses to fight for humanity's survival.

Saitama

From: One Punch Man

Saitama is a hero who can defeat any opponent with a single punch. Despite his immense power, he struggles with the boredom and lack of challenge in his fights.

Edward Elric

From: Fullmetal Alchemist

Edward Elric is a young alchemist who seeks the Philosopher's Stone to restore his brother's body. He and his brother Alphonse embark on a journey filled with danger and discovery.

Sailor Moon

From: Sailor Moon

Sailor Moon, also known as Usagi Tsukino, is a magical girl who fights evil forces to protect the Earth. She is part of a group of Sailor Scouts who defend the planet from various threats.

Levi Ackerman

From: Attack on Titan

Levi Ackerman is humanity's strongest soldier, known for his exceptional combat skills and leadership. He plays a crucial role in the fight against the Titans.

Spike Spiegel

From: Cowboy Bebop

Spike Spiegel is a bounty hunter traveling through space with his crew. Known for his laid-back attitude and martial arts skills, Spike takes on dangerous missions to make a living.
